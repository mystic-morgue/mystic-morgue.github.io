What’s in the Demo?

An introduction to the heart of our design, featuring selected levels that highlight the core gameplay and narrative, along with a sneak peek at some experimental mechanics we’re working on to expand the full game experience (found towards the end of the demo).

This demo is a work in progress. While we’ve poured a lot of care into it, you might encounter some bugs or areas that still need polishing. We truly appreciate your patience and feedback—it’s key to bringing this adventure to its full potential.

Developer mode is enabled, allowing you to move forward and backward easily. The demo includes only a few levels, with an estimated playtime of 15–20 minutes.


What Are We Looking For?

We’re passionate about making games, but we know that having a great product is only part of the equation for reaching players and succeeding. With our current team, there are areas beyond our expertise that require specialized knowledge. That’s why we’re seeking a team that can guide us in the areas where we need support.

There’s a lot we can improve on, and we’re open to discussing offers for services like QA testing, technical support, porting, or anything else. However, our main priorities are:

  • A team that understands our needs and can help us reach as many people as possible within our target audience.

  • Support with store optimization and monetization strategies for platforms like Steam, Google Play Store, and the App Store.

  • Ensuring flexibility and autonomy in the development process.

  • Opening doors and guiding us through aspects we might not have considered yet.
