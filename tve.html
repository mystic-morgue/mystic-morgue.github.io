The Visitor Effect

: Not an experiment

Steam Page


The Visitor Effect is a puzzle-adventure game that challenges the player’s creativity and problem-solving skills with unique and inventive scenarios. It avoids the common pitfall of equating complexity with fun, focusing instead on delivering the rewarding feeling of a 'eureka moment'.

The game uses a narrative to guide the player through a world where puzzle-solving seamlessly blends with exploration and adventure. This interplay allows us to control the pacing, immersing the player in an experience that feels more dynamic and engaging than simply tackling a sequence of 100 puzzles. The result is a game that stands out as a polished, elevated experience within its genre.

The Visitor Effect is a puzzle-adventure in a fictional world with a bird's eye view and unique gameplay. Guide "R3D," an alien robot, to escape the simulation he was trapped in after crashing his UFO in a wealthy nation. Here, appearances deceive, and competing interests overshadow ethics.

Dive into a captivating blend of puzzle-adventure gameplay, enriched with compelling dialogues and an array of diverse rooms waiting to be explored. Encounter enemies with varied behaviors and conquer challenging puzzles throughout your journey. But beware—a lingering sense of mystery prevails, leaving you questioning who to trust for the full story.
